**Nexomedis®** is a health-tech startup co-founded in 2024, dedicated to bridging the gap between laboratory AI research and clinical practice.

**Role**: Co-Founder & Chief Scientific Officer (CSO)

**Key activities:**
- Development of custom AI solutions (Deep Learning, LLMs, Computer Vision) for clinical diagnostics
- Ensuring scientific robustness and regulatory compliance
- Supported by the Deeptech Grant (Appel à projet Deeptech) and the DECA-BFC regional incubator program

**Award:** Winner of "Les Entrep' 2025" innovation prize, led a multidisciplinary team of engineering students (ESEO, Polytech Dijon, ESTP)
