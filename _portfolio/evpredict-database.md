A relational database engineered for the INSERM U1231 (HSP-pathies team) research group led by Dr. J. Gobbo at CGFL.

**Purpose:** Centralize storage, streamline analysis, and ensure quality control for data generated from small extracellular vesicle (sEV) research across multiple oncology projects (melanoma, sarcoma, neuroendocrine tumors, lymphoma, lung cancer).

**Technologies:** Relational database design, data quality control pipelines, machine learning–based analysis workflows
